Hello there.

Who am I?

A programmer with humble beginnings.

Specialising in object-oriented programming, and front-end work, including stylesheets, XML(Compose) & XAML(WinUI) layouts, and UX design / prototyping, as well as miscellaneous product designs, optionally.

My desire for programming stems not from corporate desires, but the eagerness to "know" and learn what can be and what cannot be. Thus, my work ethics incorporate modern approaches with an equal mix of humanism.

Why programming?

For me, programming is less of a hobby and more like a construction site. Each class is a prefabricated module, each interface could be compared to a load‑bearing beam; when they’re assembled, the architecture rises. Functional and elegant, without the need for scaffolding. The discipline of arranging these components is like the satisfaction of laying bricks in a wall. Every layer supports the next, and the structure only stands when every piece is correctly aligned.
